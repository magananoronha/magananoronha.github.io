  • mental load relief of having a secure paycheck and not having to hustle to string together grants to pay for basics like your salary, a laptop, resources for your field, lab, and computational experiments
  • companies go thru cycles of good times and bad, but even the bad times are less competitive and frugal than the best times in academia
  • limiting factor in academia is dollars, in tech it's time
